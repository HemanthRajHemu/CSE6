To produce each 100kg of Tomato and Mango and to produce each 80kg of Potato a sum of Rs.12.50 has to be used for manure. Labour required for each acre to raise the crop is 6 man-days for Tomato and Potato each and 5 man-days for Mango. A total of 500 man-days of labour at a rate of Rs.40/- per man day are available. Formulate this as a LP model to maximize the agriculturist's total profit. (10 Marks) (June/July 2018)
